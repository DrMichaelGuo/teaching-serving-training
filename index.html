Tennis Serving Position Rules

Basic Position Rules:

  • Deuce Side Position: Stand on the right side of the center mark when the total points are even (0, 2, 4, etc.)
  • Ad Side Position: Stand on the left side of the center mark when the total points are odd (1, 3, 5, etc.)
  • Always Diagonal: From either position, you'll always serve diagonally across the court

Score Examples:

  • 0-0, 15-15, 30-30: Stand on the deuce side (right side)
  • 15-0, 0-15, 30-15: Stand on the ad side (left side)
  • 40-40 (deuce): Stand on the deuce side (right side)
  • Advantage: Stand on the ad side (left side)
